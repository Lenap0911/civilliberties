Understanding the Scores

Trust in Legal System

Measured on a scale of 0-10, where:

  • 0 = No trust at all
  • 10 = Complete trust

Based on responses to the European Social Survey question about trust in the legal system.

Civil Liberties Score

Based on Freedom House's assessment of:

  • Freedom of Expression and Belief
  • Associational and Organizational Rights
  • Freedom of Assembly
  • Rule of Law
  • Personal Autonomy Personal Autonomy and Individual Rights
